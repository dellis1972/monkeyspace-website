Registration

Remember, registration starts at 8:00 AM on Oct 17. When you arrive at Microsoft Cambridge and the NERD Center, you may have to sign in at the security desk. If you have an iPhone and have upgraded to iOS 6, you can fill out this form to add your ticket to Passbook.
