Help Support Code Camp SDQ

Today we're writing to ask for your help with one of the main missions that Monkey Square was created for. That mission, is to evangelize cross platform and open-source development in .NET. We've started that mission with MonkeySpace and we're expanding that this year to help with Code Camp SDQ.

The CodeCampSDQ technical conference is a biannual conference taking place during the summer and winter, in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic. It intends to educate the community of IT professionals of the Dominican Republic and foment a spirit of knowledge sharing and collaboration at no cost to the attendees. The event is organized by Megsoft Consulting, Inc.

The last event took place on December 1, 2012, with 249 attendees (due to venue and budget limitations), and over 600 signups before registration was closed. They held 4 concurrent tracks, with 3 time slots during 1 day, for a total of 12 Sessions.
